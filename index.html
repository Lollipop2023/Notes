For headings, you have the option of h1-h6. From 1 to 6 they decrease in size. The open p is for paragraphs within the page.

inside a paragraph you add the less than sign and the lower case i to create italicized script. to go back to regular text close out the italics.

bold is a b instead of an i

and for superscript you replace i or b with the letters sup.

to add an unrendered comment INSIDE your code to talk to other coders you use the less than sign, exclamation point and then two dashes to open the comment. The closing after the comment is two dashes and the greater than sign.
